User Data Deletion for CivicPulse

Effective Date: November 2025

1. Overview

CivicPulse is a research and analytics platform that analyzes publicly available data from Facebook Pages. We do not collect, store, or process any personal user information beyond what is made available through the Facebook Graph API as public content.

2. How to Request Data Deletion

If you have connected your Facebook Page to CivicPulse and wish to remove any related data from our system, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to shahindiu16@gmail.com with the subject line "CivicPulse Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include your Facebook Page name and associated email address.
  3. Our team will verify and remove any associated data (if stored) within 7 working days.

3. Automatic Deletion

In general, CivicPulse does not permanently store personal or private Facebook data. Any temporary analytical data is automatically deleted after processing or after 30 days.
